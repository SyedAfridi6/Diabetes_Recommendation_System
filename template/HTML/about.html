About Our Diabetes Recommendation System

Our Diabetes Recommendation System is designed to provide **personalized health recommendations** based on patient data. Using **Deep Learning (DFM, LSTM)**, it predicts diabetes levels and suggests suitable diet and exercise plans tailored to each user.

How It Works?

  1. Users enter health details (BMI, BGL, Age).
  2. The AI model predicts **diabetes severity levels**.
  3. Based on the level, it recommends **diet and exercise plans**.

Key Features

  • ✔ AI-Powered **Diabetes Prediction**
  • ✔ **Diet Plans** for maintaining blood sugar levels
  • ✔ **Exercise Suggestions** based on severity
  • ✔ **Secure Login** & Data Storage

Why Choose Us?

With advanced **AI models** and a user-friendly interface, our system ensures accurate predictions and tailored recommendations, helping users take control of their health effectively.
